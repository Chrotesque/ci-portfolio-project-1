Process

I'm generally open to new comissions at any point. You can take a quick glance at my Trello page to gauge availability.

Depending on workload it might take up to a week for finish the project.

All images are typically drawn in 300 dpi ready for printing and merchandise purposes. To get there:

  1. Get in contact with me and we'll discuss what you want, the style, etc.
  2. I will prepare 2-3 sketches and you choose your favourite
  3. Ideally this is when we discuss any changes that need to be made before the majority of the work is being done
  4. Once a sketch has been agreed upon, I'll start with colorization, shading, etc.
  5. Small changes can still be made during this step but are typically a lot more involved - which is why an extra fee might be required
  6. Once finished, you'll receive a lower quality version with a watermark for approval
  7. If you're happy and the payment is done, I'll send you the final pictures in a lossless format as required
